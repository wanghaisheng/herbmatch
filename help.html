How to Play

Game Basics

Herb Match is a match-3 style game where you swap adjacent herbs to create matches of 3 or more identical herbs in a row or column.

Scoring

  • Match 3 herbs: 100 points
  • Match 4 herbs: 200 points
  • Match 5 herbs: 500 points
  • Match 6 herbs: 1000 points

Levels

Each level has a target score. Reach the target before time runs out to advance to the next level.

You'll receive a time bonus of 10 seconds when you complete a level.

Tips

  • Look for potential matches before making your move
  • Focus on creating matches of 4+ herbs for more points
  • If no moves are available, the board will automatically shuffle
